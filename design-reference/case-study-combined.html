Case study hover · B + C combined

Color awakens, then the loupe rewards.

At rest the cards are calm and slightly muted. Hover the thumbnail and three things happen in sequence: image brightens to full color, an orange light sweeps across, and a cursor loupe appears that magnifies wherever you point. Move the cursor inside the thumbnail to inspect details. The title shifts to orange. Move out to reset.

How it works: the loupe shows the same image as the thumbnail, scaled 2.4× and positioned to follow the cursor. It updates the displayed text on the fly — when you build the real version, the loupe's background URL is generated per-card so each card magnifies its own thumbnail. The label "Look closer" sits just below the loupe in cream-colored pill, so it doesn't compete with the orange ring.
